How to use

Use reading support only when you need it, move through lessons with their local maps, and know when answers are checked or submitted.

For recommended study order, use the Curriculum roadmap.

Reading settings

Open the floating gear on a study page.

The gear opens the page’s reading controls. Choose Show all readings when unfamiliar kanji block the whole page. Hide all readings also hides readings opened one word at a time. Choose Hide English to read the Japanese first, then show English again to check your interpretation.

をします。

I will clean.

Word details

Open help for one word without revealing everything.

Highlighted Japanese words have a dotted underline. Hover or focus one on desktop, or tap it on mobile, to see its reading, contextual meaning, dictionary form, kanji notes, and exact lesson occurrence. Select its vocabulary link when you want the full record. Filter the bank by part of speech or by its Part 1 grammar point.

Open the searchable vocabulary bank

Lesson navigation

Use the local map and Previous or Next controls.

Every lesson, group, and passage page has a local rail. Its links jump to source topics, book exercises, or expanded practice on that page. Previous and Next controls move through topics; the floating lesson navigation moves to the neighboring lesson or review.

The global Study menu returns to the four part landing pages when you need a broader view.

Practice behavior

Book sets and Expanded practice use different checking modes.

Book exercises and reviews
Complete the set, then submit. Feedback and rationales appear after the whole set is submitted.
Expanded practice
Check answers individually and retry immediately. Each question can be checked and reset without waiting for the rest of the set.
Full Mock
Answer all thirteen questions before submitting the complete attempt.

Open the assessment index

Reset behavior

Nothing is saved.

This static reviewer does not use accounts, local storage, cookies, analytics, or a backend. Reading visibility, hidden English, selected answers, feedback, and scores reset when you refresh or leave the page.

Use each quiz’s reset control for another attempt on the same page. Record scores in your own notebook if you want to track progress between sessions.
