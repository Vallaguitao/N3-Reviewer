Reading controls

Use help without turning it into a crutch

  • One word: hover, focus with the keyboard, or tap a dotted-underlined word to reveal only that reading.
  • Whole page: choose Show all readings when too many unknown kanji are blocking comprehension.
  • Reading challenge: choose Hide English, read the Japanese, then reveal the translation to check your interpretation.
  • Vocabulary details: select a supported word to see its dictionary form, part of speech, kanji notes, and lesson link.

Nothing is saved

This reviewer does not use accounts, local storage, cookies, analytics, or a backend. Reading settings, hidden English, answers, and scores reset when you refresh or leave the page.

If you want a record, write your score and the grammar points you missed in your own notebook after each session.
