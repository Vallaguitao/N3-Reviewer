Book functions 1–4 · page 140

1．加えて言う

To add information.

  • ノートパソコンはを。また、にもだ。
  • Laptops don't take up much space. It's also convenient to carry.
  • はをも。さらに、にケーキも。
  • He ate enough food for two people. We also ate cake after the meal.
  • がだ。そのうえ、アルバイトもしている。
  • It's hard to study every day. Besides, I also have a part-time job.
  • はが。しかも、だ。
  • Mr. Ogawa works quickly. And it's accurate.
  • を。それも、3もだ。
  • I was forced to rewrite my essay. That's three times.

2．結果・結論を言う

To express a result or conclusion.

  • があった。そのため、がいる。
  • There was an accident. As a result, the road is congested.
  • このにはがある。したがって、このはとは。
  • This research method is flawed. Therefore, this result cannot be said to be correct.
  • にがきた。そこで、だなをことにした。
  • There are more books in the room. So I decided to buy a bookshelf.
  • にレモンを。すると、が。
  • I put lemon in my tea. Then the color changed.

3．理由・根拠を言う

To express a reason or grounds for doing something.

ではをしたい。なぜなら／なぜかというと、になりたいからだ。

I want to study literature at university. Because/The reason is that I want to be a writer.

4．別の言い方で言う

To indicate an alternative way of expressing something.

このはとがだ。つまり、だ。

This job is closed on Saturdays and Sundays. That means two days off per week.

Book functions 5–9 · pages 140–141

5．比べて言う

To compare, or express a contrast.

は。それ／、はおとなしい。

My younger sister is cheerful. In contrast, my older sister is quiet.

6．どちらかであることを言う

To choose among alternatives.

のはメールでください。または、ファクスでもいいです。

Please let us know your attendance by email. Or you can fax it.

7．前の文から予想されることと違うことを言う

When an event or situation expected in a previous statement does not occur.

  • にをかけた。だが、だれも。
  • I called the house, but no one answered.
  • はもうにいるはずだ。ところが、まだもがない。
  • He should have arrived at the airport by now. However, I haven't heard anything yet.

8．条件・例外などを加える

To express reservations, a disadvantage, a condition, or an exception.

  • このアパートはとてもいい。ただ、が。
  • This apartment is very nice. However, the rent is high.
  • にしたらだ。ただし、ながあればのに。
  • If you are absent from the exam, you will fail. However, if there are special reasons, you can take the test on another day.

9．話題を変える

To change the subject.

いいにがよかったですね。ところで、ごはおですか。

I'm glad you got a job at a good company. By the way, how is your family doing?
