A simple session

Learn → Review → Practice → Mock test

  1. Learn: preview the lesson words, then study one grammar point and its ten examples.
  2. Review: hide English and reread the Japanese before revealing help.
  3. Practice: answer the three guided checks and read why every distractor fails.
  4. Mock test: finish the lesson review or the mixed 20-item set.

Lesson controls: use the lesson map or Previous and Next buttons to change topics, tap highlighted Japanese words for vocabulary details, and open Reading settings with the gear button for furigana and English controls.

Nothing is saved: readings, hidden translations, answers, and scores reset when you refresh or leave.
